27.6788° N, 84.4310° E — Bharatpur, Nepal

012.1 — Bagar Restaurant · Rapti riverbank, Chitwan · Built 2024 · Photograph: site, as found

Bagar is Nepali for the gravel bank a river leaves behind. The restaurant keeps the Rapti's bank as it was found — terraces follow the existing grade, raw log columns carry the roof beneath hand-sculpted capitals, and river stone is set straight into the plinth. The site still reads as riverbank first, building second.

Studio

The work is raw and minimal — shaped by climate, connectivity, and the quiet logic of the landscape. Buildings arrive with minimal intervention, and their comfort comes from light, materials, proportion, and airflow.

What a site already is — its grade, its stones, its trees — stays in the drawing. The practice serves private clients, hospitality operators, and institutions across the Chitwan Valley.
